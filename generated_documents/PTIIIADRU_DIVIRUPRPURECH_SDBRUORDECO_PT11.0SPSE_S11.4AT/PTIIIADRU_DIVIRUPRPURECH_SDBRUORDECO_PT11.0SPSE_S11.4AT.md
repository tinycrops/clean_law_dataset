### Sec. 11.4. - Attendance.

Council Members are expected to be present at all Special Sessions.



Sec. 11.4.1. Notice.

When a Council Member is unable to attend a Special Session, the Member shall notify
the President and the City Clerk, in writing, at least 24 hours prior to the session.
The City Clerk shall record the attendance roster for each Special Session.
